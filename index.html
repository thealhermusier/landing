What are you expecting for the perfect trip ?

1. The principle

Preparing and organizing your trip yourself requires a lot of time, but also an in-depth knowledge of the destination, the resources available on site and their accessibility. Also, lovers of traveling in complete freedom will find real partners with travel agents who will be able to advise them and take care of all the stewardship for them (selection of itineraries, reservations of accommodation, transport, excursions, guides, translators, etc.). .), highly time-consuming and often thankless task but oh so crucial to make the most of your trip..
